Language Education Research

Research Project - Eggbun Education - 2017

Company
Eggbun Education is a company that teaches indivduals Korean, Japanese or Mandarin languages through a mobile app.
Problem
Eggbun Education was relying on assumptions when making key decisions about marketing, content, and product features.
Solution
I conducted research to gain an understanding of the common needs and pain points of language learners.
Outcome
The research I conducted helped decision making throughout the company including marketing material, type of content in lessons, and product features.
Duration
2 weeks
My Role
Product Manager – My responsibilities included Recruiting | User Interviews | Surveys | User Personas | Presentation
